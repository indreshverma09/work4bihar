Vision & Goals

Bihar has suffered at the hands of injustice and ignorance for quite a long time. No leader was ever able to voice Bihar’s concerns on a national level hence causing the state to plunge into an eternal state of confusion, poverty, illiteracy, low-quality healthcare, poor irrigation and agriculture. Work For Bihar is focussed on creating concrete methods to foster economic, technological, and societal advances so that it impacts Bihar’s ecosystem for the better.

We have visualized an innovation in technology, increase in manufacturing & national trade, business & industry growth, widespread & advanced healthcare, and education through fervent efforts and practical applications. To achieve all this, we are partnering with institutions, schools, hospitals and men with a vision to push Bihar towards a better and sustainable future.

Our goal dictates giving birth to innovation and sustainable solutions to revive the long-stagnant progress of Bihar and reinforce development. We intend to draw the attention of the political parties, volunteers and patrons who can support our cause to help Bihar rise again. In order to help strengthen Bihar and ward off unemployment, illiteracy, poverty, inequality, and human strife, we need to rally like-minded individuals who want to make a difference. Your contributions and help will forever resonate with the innocent, impoverished and the illiterate population of Bihar whom you would empower using your skills and determination. Let us join forces to work for Bihar for innovative advances.
