Employment

Grade A education is one of the foundations of growth and prosperity of a nation. In Bihar, emphasis on providing primary education has yielded good results across social categories. Over the years, female participation in education is also noticed thus suppressing the belief that only male children were supposed to read and write. We emphasize our concern for building residential schools and incentivising the ones who can play a fundamental role in increasing the quality of education in the state. The immediate government has done a considerable job to equip students with school, dresses, books and study material, but they somehow missed on the quality of education that is vital for the development of the child in an elementary phase.

A majority of the students who embark on the journey to studying give up soon enough before reaching secondary education or higher education in India. The numbers in Bihar is no better.

We plan to take several initiatives that will help expand the education scene in Bihar while giving foremost importance to teaching methods, curriculum, in-school physical and mental activities, sports, and academic aptitude.
